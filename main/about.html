A little bit of info about me

I graduated University with a degree in Multimedia & Internet Technologies back in 2009 and ever since I have been working as a full time Front End Web Developer. I have worked for design agencies & also for in-house development teams during my career. Currently I am working with a fantastic team at Ocean Finance.

In my current role I work closely with other Front End Developers, Backend Developers & Project Managers to improve the Ocean Finance website and Loan/Credit Card Application forms we have built. I manage my workload using Azure and I do my development using Visual Studios 2019 (.net) and Visual Studio Code.

My core skillset covers a broad range of languages across the front end development spectrum. In recent times I have been working a lot more with JavaScript (native) and with Vue.js for front end applications, however my strength will always be in creating lightning fast reliable Wordpress websites.
